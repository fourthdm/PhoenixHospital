About Us


Phoenix Hospital - Your Trusted Health Partner in Lohegaon

Phoenix Hospital is a newly established multispecialty healthcare center committed to delivering advanced medical care with compassion and integrity. Strategically located in Lohegaon, Pune, our hospital is built with the vision to provide world-class treatment at accessible costs right within your neighborhood.

With a blend of modern infrastructure, experienced doctors, and round-the-clock medical support, we aim to set a new standard in community healthcare.
From primary consultations to critical care and complex surgeries, we ensure every patient receives personalized attention and transparent treatment.



Our Mission

To provide ethical, affordable, and quality healthcare that meets the needs of every individual and family.



Our Vision

To be the most trusted healthcare provider in the region, known for our patient-centric approach and commitment to excellence.
